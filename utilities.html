CarMaintenanceApp, FitnessHabit, TaiChiRoutine

Platform: iOS (SwiftUI)

Purpose: Three "personal utility" apps for routine tracking:

  • CarMaintenanceApp: Track your car's service, costs, and reminders.
  • FitnessHabit: Track daily habits/workouts.
  • TaiChiRoutine: Displays Tai Chi routines, steps, and timers.

App Store Connect Notes

  • Category: Lifestyle / Health & Fitness
  • Age rating: 4+
  • Privacy: Local-only, no accounts or external sync; “No data collected”
Privacy Policy: Routine and log data remains on device, private to the app user only.
