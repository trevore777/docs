Fitness / Workout Tracker

Platform: watchOS + iOS (SwiftUI)

Purpose: Track workouts, heart rate, steps, distance, energy using HealthKit APIs.

Key features / Frameworks

  • HealthKit integration (HKWorkoutSession, HKLiveWorkoutBuilder)
  • Reads and writes health data (workouts, heart rate, steps, etc.)

App Store Connect Notes

  • Category: Health & Fitness
  • Age rating: 4+
  • Privacy: Health data collected and linked to user (all stays on-device unless exported)
  • Uses NSHealthShareUsageDescription & NSHealthUpdateUsageDescription in Info.plist
Privacy Policy: All health data stays on the device; required HealthKit permission text is provided.
