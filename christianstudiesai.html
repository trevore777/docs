ChristianStudiesAI

Platform: iOS (SwiftUI)

Purpose: AI-powered Christian Studies classroom app for Years 7–12. Features AI chat, lesson timer, colouring & activities.

Key features / Frameworks

  • Network calls to AI API (student prompts + lesson text sent to server)
  • No login/accounts – lesson content stored locally (JSON)
  • Interactive activities

App Store Connect Notes

  • Category: Education (primary), Reference (secondary)
  • Age rating: 9+ or 12+ (due to internet/AI-generated content)
  • Privacy:
    • “User Content” (student questions) sent to 3rd-party AI API
    • Marked as "Data collected but not linked to user" (no accounts, no identifiers; subject to not storing IDs)
  • URLs: Needs a Privacy Policy URL
  • TestFlight: Marketing copy, screenshots, and icon prompts defined
Privacy Policy: The app does not store personal info or identifiers; student questions sent to an AI API for educational interaction.
