Granddad Assist is a simple, calming family organiser designed especially for grandparents and older adults. Keep track of children, grandchildren, and extended family members, record birthdays and important events, and build a visual family tree that makes relationships easy to remember. Add photos and videos to special occasions, write notes, and create beautiful collages that can be shared with family and friends. Granddad Assist is built with simplicity in mind: • No accounts or logins • No subscriptions • No social feeds • No tracking or analytics • All data stays on your device Whether it’s remembering birthdays, organising family gatherings, or proudly sharing memories, Granddad Assist helps you stay connected — without the complexity. Perfect for grandparents, retirees, and anyone who wants a gentle, easy-to-use family memory app. • Simple family member management • Visual family tree with relationships • Birthday and event reminders • Photo and video memories for events • Create and share memory collages • Backup and restore when changing devices • Designed for older users • Large text and calm design # Granddad Assist — Privacy Policy **Last updated:** 30 January 2026 Granddad Assist is designed to be simple and private. This app does not require an account and does not collect analytics. ## Information We Collect Granddad Assist **does not collect** personal information from you. - No account details - No tracking identifiers - No analytics - No advertising data ## Information Stored on Your Device All information you enter is stored **locally on your device**, including: - Family member details (names, relationships, birthdays, notes) - Events (titles, dates, attendees, notes) - Photos and videos you choose to attach to events - Backup files you export (if you choose to export) Granddad Assist does not upload your information to any server. ## Sharing If you choose to share a collage, photo, video, or exported backup, Granddad Assist uses the iOS Share Sheet and shares only what **you select**, to apps and people **you choose**. ## Third-Party Services Granddad Assist does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. ## Data Deletion You can delete people, events, and media from within the app. Deleting items removes them from the app’s local storage. ## Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: **Trevor Elliott** Email: **support@yourdomain.com**
