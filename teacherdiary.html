TeacherDiary App

Platform: iOS (SwiftUI)

Purpose: Teacher diary records, imports Compass CSV calendar, notes, email flows to RTC and parents.

Key features / Frameworks

  • Imports CSV calendar exported from Compass
  • Stores timetable & notes locally
  • Emails to RTC and parents

App Store Connect Notes

  • Category: Productivity / Education
  • Age rating: 4+ (staff-only)
  • Privacy: Similar to StudentTracker: local storage only, “No data collected”
  • TestFlight: Tester instructions for calendar import & email-flows
Privacy Policy: All data remains on device; emails sent only by direct user action.
